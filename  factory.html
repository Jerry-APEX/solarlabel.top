Technical & Compliance

Are your labels compliant with AS/NZS 5033:2021?

Yes, 100%. Our 46-Piece Premium Kit is specifically designed to meet AS/NZS 5033:2021 Amendment 2 requirements. This includes:

  • UV stable materials for outdoor use.
  • Correct text height (e.g., 'PV ARRAY DC ISOLATOR' > 5mm).
  • Durable adhesive that withstands Australian heat.
  • Mandatory green reflective PV circular signs (100mm).
Do you support Victorian (ESV) requirements?

Yes. We are very familiar with Energy Safe Victoria (ESV) add-ons. If you are in VIC, simply request our "VIC Compliant Pack" which includes the extra reflective signage and specific battery room labeling required by local inspectors.

What material do you use? Will it fade?

We use External Grade Traffolyte (ABS/Acrylic cap). Unlike cheap printed vinyl stickers that fade in 12 months, our text is physically engraved into the plastic. It is UV stable and guaranteed not to fade for 10+ years. We use genuine 3M 467MP adhesive to ensure they never peel off inverters.

Customization & OEM

Can I put my company logo on the kit?

Absolutely. This is our specialty. For orders over 100 sets, we can print your company logo, contact number, and website on the header card of the packaging bag for free. This is great for branding your installs.

I have a specific Shutdown Procedure. Can you match it?

Yes. Just send us a photo, PDF, or Excel file of your required text. We will create a digital proof for you to approve before mass production. We can handle complex procedures for hybrid inverters (Fronius Gen24, Sungrow, etc.).

Ordering & Logistics

What is the Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)?

Our general MOQ is 50 Sets. You can mix and match (e.g., 30 Residential Kits + 20 Battery Kits). This ensures the shipping cost per unit remains low enough to give you a great margin.

How long does delivery take to Australia?
  • Production: 3-5 working days.
  • Express Air (DHL/FedEx): 5-7 days (Recommended for small-medium orders).
  • Sea Freight: 15-25 days (Recommended for bulk orders > 500 sets for maximum savings).
What are your payment terms?

For sample orders, we accept PayPal. For wholesale production orders, we accept T/T (Bank Transfer): 30% deposit to start production, and 70% balance before shipping (after we send you photos of the finished goods).
